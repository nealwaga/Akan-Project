What This Application Is About

Hello there!

What my application basically does is: take your birth-date and calculates which day of the week you were born, and depending on your gender output, your Akan name will be displayed.

Akan names are derived from Ghanian culture. Frequently in Ghana, children are given their first names as a 'day name' which corresponds to the day in the week they were born.
